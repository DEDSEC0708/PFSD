Janmashtami Festival

Janmashtami celebrates the birth of Lord Krishna, a beloved incarnation of Lord Vishnu. It is observed with deep devotion and enthusiasm.

Devotees fast on this day and sing devotional songs. Many participate in plays and dramas that reenact scenes from Krishna's life, including his mischievous childhood antics and divine miracles.

Dahi Handi, a thrilling tradition, involves young men forming human pyramids to break a pot filled with curd, a favorite food of the young Krishna. The festival is a time of joy, music, and celebration.
